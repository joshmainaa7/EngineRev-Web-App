History of Rolls Royce

Founder: Henry Royce

Early Years:
BMW's origins trace back to March 7, 1916, when it was initially established as an aircraft engine manufacturer under the name Bayerische Flugzeugwerke AG (Bavarian Aircraft Works). It later evolved into Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, focusing on motorcycle and automobile production. Franz Josef Popp played a crucial role in the company's early development.

Expansion and Innovation: BMW has continuously expanded its product portfolio to encompass a diverse range of vehicles, including luxury sedans, coupes, convertibles, SUVs, and electric cars. The company has been a pioneer in automotive technology, consistently integrating advancements in aerodynamics, engine efficiency, and materials to elevate both performance and sustainability.
